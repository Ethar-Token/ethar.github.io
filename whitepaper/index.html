Executive Summary

ETHAR is a participation-based digital rewards platform that allows users to earn rewards by engaging with rewarded advertisements. Every completed advertisement earns tickets that users can choose to enter into daily prize draws for the opportunity to win cash rewards.


In addition to participating in prize draws, every ticket entered earns ETHAR Tokens, creating a long-term ecosystem where participation generates both immediate and future value.


Our goal is to create one of the world's largest reward communities where users can earn without making mandatory deposits while businesses gain access to highly engaged audiences through rewarded advertising.

Vision

ETHAR was created to make digital rewards simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

Reward Participation

Every action on ETHAR should provide value back to the community.

Global Access

Anyone with internet access should be able to participate regardless of location.

Fair Rewards

Every ticket entered has an equal opportunity of winning.

Long-Term Ecosystem

ETHAR Tokens create an additional layer of rewards beyond cash prizes.

How ETHAR Works

Step 1

Create an ETHAR account.

Step 2

Watch rewarded advertisements to earn tickets.

Step 3

Choose how many tickets you wish to enter into the current daily draw.

Step 4

Each ticket entered provides:

  • One entry into the daily draw.
  • 0.1 ETHAR Token.

Step 5

At the end of every day, winners are selected randomly and cash prizes are credited directly to their ETHAR Wallet.

ETHAR Token

ETHAR Token is the native digital reward token of the ETHAR ecosystem. Rather than being mined through computational power, ETHAR is earned by actively participating on the platform.


For every ticket entered into a daily draw, users receive 0.1 ETHAR. This ensures that every ticket contributes not only to the chance of winning a cash prize but also to building a growing ETHAR Token balance.

Examples

10 Tickets Entered = 1 ETHAR

100 Tickets Entered = 10 ETHAR

1,000 Tickets Entered = 100 ETHAR

As platform activity grows, ETHAR Tokens are intended to become an increasingly important part of the ecosystem.

Referral Program

Every ETHAR user receives a unique referral code and referral link.


When someone registers using your referral link, they become your lifetime referral.

Referral Rewards

You earn 10% of every prize your referrals win.

There is currently no limit to the number of people you can invite.

The referral program is designed to reward users who help grow the ETHAR community while keeping participation fair for everyone.

Daily Prize Draws

ETHAR currently operates daily prize draws where participants compete for real cash rewards.

Entry

One ticket equals one entry into the current daily draw.

Selection

Winning tickets are selected randomly after each draw closes.

Prize Pool

Winners

Fairness & Transparency

ETHAR is committed to maintaining a fair and transparent rewards platform.

  • Platform rules are applied consistently to all users.

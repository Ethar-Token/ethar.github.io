Executive summary

ETHAR is a mobile-first token distribution platform that mints virtual token balances via short, repeatable mining sessions activated by ad views. The model is designed to onboard mainstream users quickly while funding platform operations and preparing for a secure, KYC-backed migration to Ethereum mainnet.

1. Vision & rationale

ETHAR makes token ownership easy: simple ad-activated mobile mining sessions, a transparent referral program, and short session durations that encourage repeat engagement. Ads fund platform costs while referrals and boosts drive organic growth and retention.

2. Tokenomics

Key parameters:

  • Max supply: 100,000,000 ETHAR (fixed)
  • Initial mining pool: 80,000,000 ETHAR
  • Team allocation: 10,000,000 ETHAR (vested)
  • Treasury / liquidity / marketing: 10,000,000 ETHAR
  • Registration bonus: 5 ETHAR to both referrer and referee

Distribution and vesting details are configurable but recommended to include long-form vesting for team allocation and gradual treasury use for liquidity.

3. Mining mechanics

Mining is off-chain in the app (virtual balances) and follows simple rules:

  • Activation: Watch an ad to completion → 24-hour mining session starts.
  • Base rate: 0.2 ETHAR / hour.
  • Referral bonus: +0.05 ETHAR / hour per active referral.
  • Ad boosts: +0.05 ETHAR / hour for 6h (max 2).
  • Session: Continues if app backgrounded; stops on logout.

Rate formula:

rate = 0.2 + (active_referrals × 0.05) + (active_ad_boosts × 0.05)

4. Anti-dump & lockups

  1. 50% default lockup for 3–12 months at migration.
  2. Incentives for longer lockups.
  3. Team vesting: 24 months, 6-month cliff.
  4. Gradual liquidity release.

5. Revenue & sustainability

  • 60% operations & development
  • 25% liquidity & buybacks
  • 15% marketing & community

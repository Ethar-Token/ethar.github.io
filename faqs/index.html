Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ETHAR Rewards.

What is ETHAR?

ETHAR is a rewards platform where users can watch rewarded ads to earn tickets that can be entered into daily prize draws. Winners receive real cash rewards, and users also earn ETHAR Tokens as part of the platform ecosystem.

How do I earn tickets?

You earn tickets by watching rewarded ads. Every completed ad gives you one ticket.

How do I enter the draw?

Watching ads earns tickets, but tickets are not entered automatically. You choose how many available tickets you would like to enter into the current daily draw.

How are winners selected?

At the end of every daily draw, winners are selected randomly from all tickets entered into that draw.

How many winners are there?

Each daily draw currently selects 10 to 100 winners. The number of winners and prize distribution may change as the platform grows.

How is the prize pool calculated?

Prize pools are funded from platform advertising revenue after operational costs have been deducted.

How do I receive my winnings?

Winning amounts are credited to your ETHAR wallet. From there, you can request a withdrawal using one of the available withdrawal methods.

Do I need to deposit money?

No. The current platform allows participation without making any deposits. Tickets are earned by watching rewarded advertisements.

What are ETHAR Tokens?

ETHAR Tokens are the platform's digital reward token. They are earned through participation and are intended to provide additional utility as the ETHAR ecosystem expands.

What is the referral program?

Invite your friends using your unique referral link. You earn 10% of every prize your referrals win for as long as they remain active on the platform.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No. There is no limit to the number of referrals you can invite.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Each person is allowed only one account. Multiple accounts may result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform.

Can I transfer my ETHAR Tokens?

Token transfers and additional token utilities may become available as the ETHAR ecosystem develops.

Can I change my personal information?

Yes. You can update your name, phone number, country, date of birth, and password at any time from your Profile page.

Is my personal information secure?

We take reasonable measures to protect your account information and personal data. Never share your password with anyone.

What happens if I forget my password?

You will be able to reset your password using your registered email address. Just click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, and you will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

Can I watch ads on multiple devices?

For account security and fraud prevention, unusual activity across multiple devices may be reviewed. Always use your own account and follow the platform rules.

Why didn't I win?

Each ticket entered into a draw has an equal chance of being selected. Entering more tickets increases your chances, but every draw is random.

Will ETHAR introduce new features?

Yes. We plan to continue improving the platform by adding new rewards, token utilities, promotions, and additional ways to participate.

Still have a question?

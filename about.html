About ETHAR

Rewarding participation. Building the future.

Who We Are

ETHAR is a digital rewards platform designed to give people an opportunity to earn through simple participation. Users can watch rewarded advertisements, earn tickets, and enter hourly prize draws for the chance to win real cash rewards.


Our goal is to create a fair, transparent and enjoyable platform where advertising benefits both businesses and the community using ETHAR.

How ETHAR Works

🎥 Watch Ads

Watch rewarded ads and earn tickets.

🎟 Enter Draws

Choose how many of your earned tickets to enter into each hourly prize draw.

🏆 Win Rewards

Hourly winners receive cash rewards credited to their ETHAR wallet.

👥 Refer Friends

Invite friends and earn 10% of every prize they win for life.

Our Vision

ETHAR is more than a rewards platform. Our long-term vision is to build a complete digital ecosystem powered by the ETHAR Token. As the platform grows, we plan to introduce additional utilities, community features, and opportunities that increase the value of participating in the ETHAR ecosystem.

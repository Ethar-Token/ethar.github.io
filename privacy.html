Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

ETHAR respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the ETHAR mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, email, phone number, country, profile photo, password, and referral code.
  • Authentication & Security Data: Account ID, login timestamps, device identifiers, and authentication tokens.
  • KYC & Compliance Data: If KYC is required, government-issued ID information, selfies, and verification documents.
  • Usage Data: Mining activity, token balances, referrals, app interactions, ad engagement, and crash logs.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, operating system, app version, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App and related services.
  • To authenticate accounts, manage user sessions, and prevent fraud.
  • To process mining rewards, referral bonuses, and token transactions.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including KYC requirements.
  • To communicate important updates, promotions, and notices related to your account or App.
  • To monitor, analyze, and enhance the security, performance, and functionality of the App.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with hosting, analytics, customer support, or payment processing.
  • Legal Compliance: When required to comply with legal obligations, enforce terms of service, or protect rights and property.
  • KYC Verification: When required by regulators or exchanges to verify identity and comply with anti-money laundering laws.
  • Corporate Transactions: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers involving the App.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or as required by law. Token balances, mining history, and referral records are maintained to accurately reflect account activity.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your login credentials.

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access, correct, or update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 14 years. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover we have, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Blockchain & Token Considerations

ETHAR tokens displayed in the app are virtual and for demonstration purposes only. Token balances and transactions may be recorded on public blockchain networks in future updates. Blockchain transactions are immutable and visible to other network participants. Any personal information stored on-chain will be minimized and anonymized whenever possible.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with the effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

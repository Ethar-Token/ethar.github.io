About ETHAR Token

ETHAR Token is the digital reward token that powers the ETHAR ecosystem. Every time you participate in a daily draw, you earn ETHAR Tokens alongside your chance to win cash prizes.


For every 1 ticket entered into a draw, you receive 0.1 ETHAR. The more tickets you enter, the more ETHAR Tokens you accumulate over time.

Example:

10 Tickets Entered = 1 ETHAR
100 Tickets Entered = 10 ETHAR
1,000 Tickets Entered = 100 ETHAR

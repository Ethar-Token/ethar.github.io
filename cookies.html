ETHAR Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, improve performance, enhance security, and provide a better browsing experience.


ETHAR also uses similar technologies such as local storage, pixels, and device identifiers where appropriate.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the ETHAR platform.

  • Keep you signed into your account.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Protect your account against fraud.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Measure website traffic.
  • Support rewarded advertisements.
  • Understand how visitors use ETHAR.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose
Essential Cookies Required for login, security, and core website functionality.
Performance Cookies Help us understand website usage and improve performance.
Preference Cookies Remember settings such as language and user preferences.
Advertising Cookies Used by advertising partners to deliver and measure rewarded advertisements.
Analytics Cookies Help us understand visitor behaviour and improve the platform.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies used on ETHAR are placed by trusted third-party providers that help us operate the platform and deliver services.

  • Rewarded advertising providers.
  • Website analytics providers.
  • Cloud hosting services.
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs).
  • Fraud prevention and security services.

These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy and cookie policies. ETHAR does not control how third parties use the information they collect.

Third-party advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to verify advertisement completion, prevent fraud, limit repeated advertisements, and measure campaign performance.

5. Rewarded Advertisements

ETHAR uses rewarded advertisements to allow users to earn tickets for participation in daily prize draws.

  • Advertising partners may place cookies or similar technologies during ad delivery.
  • These technologies help confirm that advertisements were viewed successfully.
  • They also help detect fraudulent activity and prevent abuse of reward systems.
  • Advertisement availability may differ depending on your country, device, browser, and advertising partner.

ETHAR does not guarantee that rewarded advertisements will always be available to every user.

6. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through their settings.

  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Block future cookies.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.
  • Restrict cookies from specific websites.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features of ETHAR from functioning correctly, including account login and secure sessions.

7. Browser Controls

You can usually manage cookies through your browser's privacy or security settings.

Browser Cookie Controls
Google Chrome Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
Microsoft Edge Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions
Mozilla Firefox Settings → Privacy & Security
Safari Settings → Privacy

8. Cookie Consent

When you first visit the ETHAR website, you may be presented with a cookie notice requesting your consent to the use of non-essential cookies where required by applicable law.


You can change or withdraw your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or any cookie preference tools that may be provided on the website.

Essential cookies required for the operation and security of ETHAR cannot be disabled through the website because they are necessary for core functionality.

ETHAR may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, advertising partners, or our services.


When significant changes are made, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the website or other communication channels.


Your continued use of ETHAR after any changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Cookie Policy.

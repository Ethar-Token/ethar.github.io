Frequently Asked Questions

What is ETHAR?
ETHAR is a cryptocurrency mining app where users can earn ETHAR tokens through mining, referrals, and registration bonuses. It is designed to be user-friendly and secure.
How do I start mining?
Log in to the app, go to the Home page, and click "Start Mining." You must watch an ad to activate mining, which runs for 24 hours automatically. Tokens accumulate every second.
How is my mining rate calculated?
Base rate: 0.2 ETHAR/hour. It increases by +0.05 ETHAR per active referral and +0.05 ETHAR per ad boost (max 2 boosts active at a time and each ad boost runs for 6 hours only). There is no maximum mining rate.
What is the mining pool?
The mining pool starts at 80 million ETHAR tokens. Tokens are distributed via mining, referral bonuses, and successful registrations.
Can I mine all the time?
Mining lasts 24 hours per session. After the session ends, click "Start Mining" again. Mining stops on sign-out but continues if the app is closed.
How do referrals work?
Every user has a unique referral code. Share it with friends to earn 5 ETHAR when they sign up. Active referrals are those currently mining.
How do I manage my profile?
You can only update your email, phone, password, and profile photo. First and last names and country cannot be changed, if need be, kindly contact support in-app or email support@ethartoken.com. KYC and account deletion options are in the Profile page.
What is KYC and why is it required?
KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process to confirm your identity before migrating to the Mainnet. It ensures transparency and prevents fraudulent activities. The KYC process will begin soon — well before the end of the mining phase — to ensure a smooth transition to the Mainnet launch and exchange listings. Users will be required to submit valid government-issued identification when KYC becomes available in the Profile section.
When does mining end?
We are hoping for mining to end on March 20, 2026. Make sure to mine regularly to maximize your ETHAR tokens.
When will the ETHAR Mainnet launch?
The ETHAR Mainnet will launch after the KYC process begins, followed by exchange listings. This marks the transition from mining to the official blockchain network where ETHAR tokens become fully transferable.
